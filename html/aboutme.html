About Me

Hi! I'm Stephen Cannon. I love good books, great movies, and tasty food. I'm of the strong opinion that everyone should try Waffle Love's chicken and waffles at least once in their life - you can check out their website here. I also love traveling, and I'm hoping to do much, much more of it.

One of my favorite parts of traveling is trying new food. On a trip to Spain with my parents, my mom and I really enjoyed the paella that we had in Granada. My dad didn't have any since he insists he's allergic to seafood:)

I also love my dog, Sadie. She is a black lab, and she turns 12 years old this upcoming November. She has definitely gotten more mellow (and tired) as she's aged, but she's still always excited to go on a walk. She's the best dog I could ask for!
